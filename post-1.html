The Journey

Introduction

Posted by Katrina Lucas on December 15th 2020

The purpose of my autoethnography blog is to look
through the reflective lens on my experiences as a black Barbadian woman who is struggling to retain my ethnic identity and how these struggles impact my
ability to integrate in British culture.

I will commence my journey with a brief explanation
of ethnography followed by brief introduction to my cultural background and how it has shaped and molded my ethnic identity. I will guide you through my
struggles to adjust to the ethnic minority label prescribe by British culture and how this impinges my
interactions within British culture.

Moreover, I will also reflect on how my intercultural
communication impinges on my ethnic identity which brings about a bicultural identity. 

Additionally, I will explore how these struggles has
molded me as an English Language teacher. I will also draw on relevant literature to bring clarity to the elements of my struggles.

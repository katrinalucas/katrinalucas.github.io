Revelation

 

Posted by Katrina Lucas on January 15th 2021

Going through the process of writing this autoethnography, it has provided me with the opportunity to reflect on my experiences as a Black Barbadian migrant in Britain. Through these reflections I was able to glean a deeper understanding of why is struggled against the label ethnic minority and how releasing that struggles has allow me to grow as an individual. My autoethnography journey has develop a better understanding of how my culture is ingrained in the way I communicate with others also well as my identity and how these factors influence my ability to effectively communicate interculturally.

Moreover, I was also able to understand the impact the aspect of my culture such as speed of speech, dialect and accent have on the effectiveness of my intercultural communication. I believe that I have grown as an individual as a direct result of this experience.
